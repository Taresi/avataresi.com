Sample Projects

Here is some samples of my projects as a technical writer:

AI in FinTech: A Guide to Feed Data-Hungry AI with Healthy Data

AI in FinTech is a whitepaper analyzing the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and FinTech in the financial industry. AI crosses electronic frontiers to develop its services in many industries including the financial industry. Single sourcing the available data is a more affordable and more reliable way to feed AI. In this brief, the researcher reviews how digital ID systems can contribute to the AI industry and empower financial technology.

 

How to Use SEO: A Getting Started Guide for Bloggers

As a blogger you write contents, and you want your target audience to read them. Your job does not finish at writing and posting your content though; you must make the content accessible to your readers. How to Use SEO: A Getting Started Guide for Bloggers tell you how to teach the search engines what your page is about and which of your posts are relevant to the audience’s search inquiry.

 

User Guide to Dolibarr Projects/Collaborative Work Application

The User Guide to Dolibarr Project/Collaborative Work Application covers three modules of Dolibarr in “Projects/ Collaborative work” application: the Projects or Leads Module, Events/Agenda Module, and Resources Module. Each chapter of the user guide will explain one module of the application. Adobe FrameMaker is the main authoring tool of this UG.
